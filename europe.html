Economic Sentiment Indicator

In the European Union, the Economic sentiment indicator is a composite measure (average=100) that calculates the confidence level among: manufacturers (40 percent of the index); service providers (30 percent); consumers (20 percent); retailers (5 percent) and constructors (5 percent).

Business Confidence

In the European Union, the business confidence refers to the sentiment among manufacturers.
